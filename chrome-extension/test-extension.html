🐛 Debugging Steps:

If bookmarks are not organizing:

  1. Open chrome://extensions/
  2. Click "Details" on Smart Bookmark Organizer
  3. Click "Inspect views: service worker" to see console logs
  4. Look for errors or "Auto-organize is disabled" message
  5. Make sure toggle is ON in extension popup
